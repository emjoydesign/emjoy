Overview

PIMS (Patient Information Management Systems) is the platform the Lemonaid doctors used to review and prescribe patient health care requests. As the sole designer, I was responsible for designing the entire doctor-facing platform.

My Role
  • Sole designer for the doctor-facing platform
  • Designed end-to-end patient review and prescription workflows
  • Worked directly with physicians to understand clinical needs
  • Built a system that streamlined the consultation process
Context

Lemonaid Health needed an internal tool that allowed doctors to efficiently review patient questionnaires, make clinical decisions, and prescribe medications — all within a streamlined digital workflow.

Problem
  • Doctors spent too much time navigating between patient records
  • The existing system lacked structure for efficient clinical decision-making
  • No standardized workflow for reviewing and prescribing
Goal

Create a unified platform that enables doctors to review patient information, make clinical decisions, and prescribe treatments efficiently and accurately.

Approach

I worked closely with Lemonaid's medical team to understand their clinical workflows, then designed a system that mirrored their decision-making process digitally.

  • Shadowed doctors during patient consultations
  • Mapped clinical decision trees
  • Designed modular patient review interfaces
  • Built prescription workflow with safety checks
Outcome
  • Streamlined the doctor review process
  • Reduced average consultation time
  • Improved prescription accuracy through built-in safeguards
