Overview

Lemonaid partnered with Planned Parenthood to create an iOS and Android app that provides patients with birth control prescriptions, UTI treatment, and STD testing. This partnership aimed to expand access to reproductive healthcare through technology.

My Role
  • Led product design for the Planned Parenthood mobile application
  • Designed the patient intake and consultation flow
  • Ensured HIPAA compliance in all user-facing interfaces
  • Collaborated with Planned Parenthood's clinical team on medical workflows
Context

Planned Parenthood wanted to expand access to healthcare services through a digital platform. Lemonaid's telehealth infrastructure provided the technical foundation, while Planned Parenthood brought trusted brand recognition and clinical expertise.

Problem
  • Many patients face barriers to accessing reproductive healthcare in person
  • Stigma prevents some patients from seeking care
  • Geographic limitations restrict access in rural areas
Goal

Make reproductive healthcare accessible to anyone with a smartphone, providing a private, convenient, and affordable path to care.

Approach

I designed the app experience to feel approachable and trustworthy while meeting strict medical and privacy requirements.

  • Designed welcoming onboarding that reduced patient anxiety
  • Built clear, simple medical questionnaire flows
  • Created pharmacy selection and prescription tracking interfaces
  • Ensured accessibility compliance throughout
Outcome
  • Successfully launched on iOS and Android
  • Expanded access to reproductive healthcare services
  • Maintained high patient satisfaction scores
